Small stories

I actually thought deeply about coding this year because I thought it was cool to see my favorite YouTuber doing coding.
At that time, I thought that I wanted to do coding as my dream.
I thought that I wanted to be that cool person, too

Since I was little, my dream has been to become a fashion designer.
It's amazing that it suddenly changed to another dream this year.
I hope having this dream can be a good breakthrough.

I want to learn coding quickly and succeed, but I think I will need a lot of time as a beginner.
I will give up my greed and learn step by step.
In the real world, my grades are not bad, my paintings are not bad, I speak pretty well,
but I don't think I'm particularly good at anything.

I don't know if it's because of my personal greed, but this has always been my personal weakness.
The other students seemed to have already set their own paths and tried, so they were getting smaller.
But I think that's why I became more interested in coding. Now I have a way forward.
The process of learning now is not difficult, it's fun.
